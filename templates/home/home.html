Welcome to AGPT

We are team of talented developers creating article summary using Transformers

Get Started

Article Summarization using Generative Pretrained Transformer

The website will use APIs to gather articles from a variety of sources on the internet and then use a generative pre-trained transformer to produce concise summaries of the articles

Deep learning model

The deep learning model used in this project will be trained on a large corpus of news articles and summaries to accurately capture the key information and important points of each article. This training will ensure that the summarized articles provide a comprehensive understanding of the original content while still being brief and easy to digest.

Personalized and Automated Approach

The personalized and automated approach to news summarization offered by this project has the potential to revolutionize the way people stay informed and make sense of the vast amount of information available on the internet. This is especially important in today's fast-paced world where people are constantly bombarded with information from a multitude of sources.

Categories

News categories encompass a wide range of topics, including sports, entertainment, education, technology, health and lifestyle, and trade and professional, and you can select any of these categories to stay informed.

Sports

Sports news is the reporting of events and information related to competitive physical activities or games

Health & Medicine

Health and lifestyle news covers the reporting of information, trends, and events related to health, wellness, and personal habits, including nutrition, fitness, disease prevention, and environmental factors affecting health

Education

Education news refers to the reporting of events, policies, and developments in the field of education and learning, including early childhood, K-12, higher education, and lifelong learning.

Technology

Technology news covers the reporting of new developments, trends, and products in the field of technology, including computer hardware, software, electronics, and telecommunications.

Entertainment

Entertainment news is the reporting of events, trends, and personalities related to the entertainment industry, including movies, music, television, and celebrities.

Trade & Professional

Trade and professional news refers to the reporting of information, events, and developments specific to a particular industry or profession, including business and finance, law, medicine, and scientific research.
